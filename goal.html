Our Goal

The DEBUGS team is committed to building a reliable, accessible, and efficient document management system for the baranggay. Our goals drive every decision we make as a team throughout the development of this project.

Efficiency

Reduce document processing time by enabling online submission and tracking for baranggay residents.

Security

Ensure that all resident data and uploaded documents are stored securely with proper access controls.

Accessibility

Make baranggay services available to all residents anytime, anywhere through a mobile-friendly platform.

Community Impact

Empower the community by reducing the need for in-person visits and cutting down on paperwork.

Mission

To harness the power of technology to design and develop innovative systems that address real-world challenges in baranggay document management, fostering collaboration, creativity, and continuous growth as future leaders in technology.

Vision

To become recognized leaders in technology and system development, applying our knowledge and creativity to shape the future of digital solutions that empower communities and industries through transformative technologies.
