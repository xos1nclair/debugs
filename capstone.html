Capstone

The DEBUGS Baranggay Document System is a capstone project developed by students of Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Dasmariñas. It aims to digitize and streamline baranggay document requests and management for residents and baranggay officials alike.

Project Title

Baranggay Document Management System (DEBUGS)

Academic Year

2025 – 2026

Institution

Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Dasmariñas

Tech Stack

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL

Project Description

This system was developed to address the inefficiencies in manual baranggay document processing. Residents can now submit requests, upload required documents, and track the status of their applications online — reducing wait times and eliminating the need for multiple in-person visits.
